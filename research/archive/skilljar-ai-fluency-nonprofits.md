## About This Course

This free course from Anthropic, developed in partnership with GivingTuesday, teaches nonprofit professionals practical AI collaboration skills through the 4D Framework (Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence).

The training empowers nonprofit professionals to develop competency with AI tools while maintaining organizational values. Nonprofit professionals are uniquely positioned to leverage AI for social good when approaching implementation thoughtfully.

## Target Audience

- Fundraising staff
- Communications professionals
- Program delivery teams
- Operations personnel
- Leadership roles
- Nonprofit staff across all functions

## Prerequisites

- Familiarity with foundational AI concepts (ideally through the prerequisite "AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations" course)
- Access to an AI chatbot (Claude.ai used as primary example, though any tool works)

## Course Curriculum

### Module 1: Introduction and AI Fluency Framework
1. **Welcome to AI Fluency for nonprofits** - Course introduction, overview of how AI can serve the nonprofit sector
2. **The 4D Framework** - Overview of the four interconnected competencies: Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence

### Module 2: The Description-Discernment Loop
3. **Researching with AI** - Using AI for nonprofit research tasks: donor research, grant research, program evaluation research, landscape analysis
4. **Writing with AI** - Using AI for nonprofit writing tasks: grant proposals, donor communications, program reports, marketing content

### Module 3: The Delegation-Diligence Loop
5. **Understanding privacy and data** - Data privacy considerations for nonprofits using AI, protecting constituent data, ethical data handling
6. **Data analysis with AI** - Using AI for data analysis tasks common in nonprofits: donor analytics, program metrics, impact measurement

### Module 4: Putting It All Together
7. **Workflow augmentation** - Integrating AI into existing nonprofit workflows for greater efficiency and impact
8. **Integration** - Strategies for broader organizational AI integration, change management, building AI literacy across teams

### Module 5: Conclusion and Certificate
9. **Next steps** - Continuing the AI Fluency journey, additional resources
10. **Course Quiz** - Final assessment for certificate of completion

## Key Concepts Covered

- **4D Framework**: Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence
- **The Description-Discernment loop**: Research and writing with AI
  - Researching with AI (donor research, grant research, landscape analysis)
  - Writing with AI (grant proposals, communications, reports)
- **The Delegation-Diligence loop**: Privacy, data, and analysis
  - Understanding privacy and data protection for constituent information
  - Data analysis for impact measurement and donor analytics
- **Workflow augmentation**: Integrating AI into nonprofit operations
- **Organizational integration**: Change management and team AI literacy
- Maintaining organizational values while adopting AI tools
- Leveraging AI for social good
